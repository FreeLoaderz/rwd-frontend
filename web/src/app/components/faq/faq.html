SmartClaimz FAQ

FreeLoaderz SmartClaimz is a novel reward distribution platform that aims to be the premier token claiming Web3 dApp for end users. Decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs), token projects, NFT projects, video game projects, and more will be able to offer their tokens through SmartClaimz to their users.

To speed up the transactions, SmartClaimz submits them to the FreeLoaderz Load Balancers. The Load Balancers pass the transaction to Cardano Submit API enabled Relays. Each of these Relays are run by members of the FreeLoaderz Coalition.

Follow these basic instructions to claim tokens on SmartClaimz:

  • Connect your wallet using the "Connect" button in the Navigation Bar
  • Accept the Terms of Service
  • If you are delegated to a participating pool, you will see a list of tokens available.
  • Choose the tokens you want to claim
  • Click "Submit Claimz"
  • Sign the transaction
  • The tokens will appear in your wallet once the transaction is finalized by the network

More detailed instructions are available in our test net guide.

There are a couple of things you can try to get your wallet connected. If you make any changes to your wallet or browser, reload the site.

  • Clear your browser cache (typically left ctrl+shift+del to bring up the browser modal)
  • Restart your browser
  • Make sure your browser wallet extension is installed
  • Make sure DApp access is enabled in your wallet
  • Remove our site from your wallet's allowlist/whitelist

It can take 10 to 15 minutes (possibly more) for you to receive your tokens in your wallet. It'll depend on network congestion and other factors. With that, here are some things to be aware of:

  • If you didn't get the Success notification with a link to your claim, it didn't go through
  • If the transaction went through and you have waited 15 minutes, has the ADA been debited from your wallet? If not, the transaction hasn't been completed yet
  • If ADA has been debited for the transaction and you still have not received tokens, please let us know at info@smartclaimz.io and we will escalate a ticket for you

As each project has its own metrics, please reach out directly to the project team for the token for a review of the situation.

The need for collateral was introduced with the addition of smart contracts on Cardano. Setting the collateral in your wallet helps ensure that the transaction will be validated properly. Currently, most wallets recommend that you set the collateral to 5 ADA. The Vasil hard fork will bring a more deterministic method to match the exact amount needed.
